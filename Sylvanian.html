Sylvanian Families

Sylvanian Families is a popular line of plush family toys from Epoch (Japan), established in 1985, notable for its exquisite and adorable designs. The characters (rabbits, squirrels, cats, etc.) live in the Sylvanian forest, emphasizing family, nature, and friendship, suitable for both children and collectors.

Outstanding characters

  • bear family
  • chocolate rabbit family
  • bird family
  • persian cat family
